About Us

Fashion store

We started selling popular clothing in 2012. At the end of 2013, we opened our first store called "Fashion". The founders of our store are the Johnnas brothers. Our store provides the highest quality clothing made locally and from quality material. We sell only the best merchandise. Because the merchandise goes through a long inspection before it hits the shelves of our store. At the moment we have several outlets in Astana and Almaty, as well as in other cities. To date, we have sold more than 3 million pieces of clothing.
